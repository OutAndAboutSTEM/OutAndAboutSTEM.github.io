LGBT+ Wikipedia edit-a-thon



On 25 February we will organise an LGBT+-themed Wikipedia edit-a-thon. We will be learning how to edit and write Wikipedia articles and use these new skills to improve the representation of LGBT+ scientists (and other minorities) on Wikipedia! We will be joined by Wikimedian at the Wellcome Collection, Alice White, and comedian, PhD student and podcaster, Cerys Bradley.

More information and a link to sign up will be released shortly.
