Out and About in STEM

Out and About STEM is a project at the University of Sussex started by Dr Katy Petherick. Our mission is to promote the visibility of LGBT+ people in STEM. We raise the profile of LGBT+ members of staff and students during public-facing activities by wearing LGBT+ scientist badges during outreach activities. We also promote the visibility of LGBT+ members by featuring LGBT+ scientists on our social media channels. Furthermore, we have written blogs for special occasions, such as LGBT+ history month and Trans Day of Visibility, which were featured on the BioChemist blog. We received funding for this project from a Biochemical Society Diversity in Science grant.
